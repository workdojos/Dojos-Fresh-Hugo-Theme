    {{% subtitle5 "Empower employees with the ultimate open-source toolkit" %}}
Productivity shines when employees are empowered to build.  There is no better place to build in our digital age than to do so on your own server.

Work Dojo's mission is to help you make this possible.  Each Dojo is self-hosted, meaning that we equip your users with the ability to host their own server.

This includes a dashboard that they own and control.  A place to put the most relevant info for their job front and center.  It's not dictacted by a higher corporate power but rather bestowed with automony and independence.  These things are appreciated, and the toolkit goes even deeper than that.

Equip and empower your employees with open-source tech.  Work Dojos will help you make that happen.

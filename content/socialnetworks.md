With open-source technology, you absolutely can and it costs next to nothing.  We would be happy to talk to you about what it will take for you to build your own Twitter.  Free of charge of course!

Building a social network is a fun process, one that we ourselves took just 2 years ago.  We explored different open-source apps like https://www.rocket.chat/ and https://zulip.com/. https://joinmastodon.org/ and https://mattermost.com/.

Being open-source means that the source code for these apps is posted online for anyone to use.  We'd be glad to walk you through the steps of self-hosting these apps.  Any app needs its own server and Virtual Private Servers can be had for as little as $5/month for the DIY'ers out there.

If you would like a quick-start to these apps and more, every Work Dojo comes prebuilt with some of the best!  A Dojo costs a flat $25/month.
